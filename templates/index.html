About Us

Ubuntu Fund Drive is a non-profit initiative dedicated to connecting donors with underprivileged students in Kenya. Through generous contributions, we provide financial support for school fees, uniforms, shopping essentials, and mentorship programs to empower the next generation.

Our Mission: Bridging the Gap in Education

At Ubuntu Fund Drive, we believe every child deserves an education. By linking donors to needy students, we ensure that financial barriers do not stand in the way of academic success. Our initiative not only covers school fees but also provides essential learning resources and mentorship programs.

  • Providing full scholarships covering tuition, school uniforms, and shopping essentials.
  • Offering mentorship programs to guide and inspire students.
  • Creating lasting partnerships between donors and beneficiaries to foster long-term educational success.

Awards & Recognitions

Ubuntu Fund Drive has been recognized by esteemed institutions for our commitment to providing educational opportunities to underprivileged students across Kenya.

Excellence in Education Award - Alliance High School

In recognition of our dedication to supporting students from financially challenged backgrounds, Alliance High School honored Ubuntu Fund Drive for bridging the education gap through scholarships and mentorship programs.

Community Impact Award - Maseno School

Maseno School acknowledged our efforts in sponsoring needy students and providing them with educational materials, school uniforms, and personal development mentorship to ensure they thrive academically and socially.

Humanitarian Recognition - SDA Churches of Kenya

The SDA Churches of Kenya awarded us for our dedication to uplifting underprivileged students by ensuring their school fees, basic needs, and mentorship support are fully covered through donor contributions.

Education Support Excellence - Red Cross Kenya

Red Cross Kenya recognized Ubuntu Fund Drive for our impact in ensuring that vulnerable students receive the necessary financial support to stay in school and achieve their dreams, helping create a brighter future.
